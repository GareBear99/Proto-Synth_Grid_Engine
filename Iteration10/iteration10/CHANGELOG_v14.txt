Synth Grid Engine v14 — Probe Authority-Gated Pathing

Changes:
- Probe movement now follows route-graph authority pathing instead of direct free-flight.
- Free roam proposals resolve to route tiles and path through adjacency.
- Probe always treats spawn tile as canonical home anchor.
- When free roam is off, probe path-locks back to spawn and settles there.
- Added internal shortest-path routing and live path authority state.
- Probe settings panel now reports auth state.
